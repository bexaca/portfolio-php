STEP BY STEP

Step 1

After you send us a detailed inquiry about your dental care needs via contact you will find here, our team of experts will estimate the cost and length of required treatment. Also, you are able to choose amongst other services we provide (accomodation, tours, etc.).

Step 2

After we have established your Dental Dream arrangement, you will be sent a contract that you can edit if found necessary.

Step 3

Whether you travel by plane, bus or train, our staff will be at your service for transferring to your accomodation in Novi Sad.When you have settled, you will have a final check up with your dentist before commencing the treatment. Your personal schedule of appointments will be ready for you as well as the best and affordable tourist offers in Novi Sad.

Step 4

As for some dental procedures you will have a lot of free time in between, we offer you a variety of interesting tours so you can experience the very best of Novi Sad and our beautiful Serbia. In Dental Dream, you will be provided with custom-made offers for you to consider.

Step 5

After you have received your dental treatment, and had fun exploring Novi Sad with the help of our wonderful staff, you will be transferred back to the airport.
